# VidScreener: The AI-Assisted Video Evaluation Platform

VidScreener transforms unstructured video submissions into structured, explainable, and scalable assessments.

---

## The Problem

Large-scale admissions and hiring processes are often bogged down by manual video review. For example, Plaksha University’s "Young Creators League" received 500+ submissions reviewed by only 5 evaluators. Common issues include:

- **Inefficiency**: Manual downloads, Excel tracking, and fragmented data.
- **Subjectivity & Bias**: Evaluators can be swayed by video quality or scripted delivery.
- **Wasted Effort**: Poor audio/video quality wasting human reviewer time.

---

## The Solution: VidScreener

VidScreener replaces fragmented manual workflows with a unified, edge-native platform that combines multimodal AI with human expert review.

### 1. Explainable AI
We don't believe in "black-box" decisions. Every AI score is grounded with exact timestamped transcript snippets, so evaluators know exactly why a candidate scored a 4/5 on "Clarity."

### 2. Human-in-the-Loop
AI does the heavy lifting—the first-pass evaluation—while humans maintain ultimate control. What used to take 20 minutes now takes 3, with AI pre-populating scores and surfacing key insights.

### 3. Real-Time Calibration
Our analytics suite compares AI vs. Human scores in real-time, helping admins detect bias, identify rubric drift, and ensure consistency across their entire evaluation team.

### 4. Enterprise-Grade Scalability
Built on the Cloudflare global network, VidScreener handles hundreds of thousands of submissions with zero infrastructure overhead. Whether it's a small internal project or a global fellowship, VidScreener scales with you.
